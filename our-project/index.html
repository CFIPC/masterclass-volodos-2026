Our Mission

Our Mission

Established in 2013 by Belgian painter Magda Calleeuw and Belgian pianist Philippe Raskin, the mission of the César Franck International Piano Competition is to encourage new generations of young pianists to transcend their own capabilities by providing an international platform.

With around 70 participants originating from about 25 different countries and an internationally acclaimed jury in 2025, the César Franck has established itself as a leading competition for young musicians worldwide.

The incorporation of the competition, concerts and educational programs such as this very masterclass with Mto. Volodos as a yearly event symbolizes our commitment and passion to the advancement of culture and education.

Biography

Arcadi Volodos

Volodos has everything - imagination, passion and a phenomenal technique - to carry out his ideas on the piano. His limitless virtuosity, combined with a unique sense of rhythm, color and poetry, makes Volodos the narrator of intense stories and infinite worlds!

Born in St Petersburg in 1972, Arcadi Volodos began his musical studies with lessons in singing and conducting. He did not begin serious training as a pianist until 1987 at the St Petersburg Conservatory before pursuing advanced studies at the Moscow Conservatory with Galina Egiazarova and in Paris and Madrid.

Since making his New York debut in 1996, Volodos has performed throughout the world in recital and with the most eminent orchestras and conductors.
